The Project Goal

The Plant Care Reminder App is a premium responsive frontend application developed for demonstrating interactive DOM manipulation, CSS grid alignments, and offline visual asset implementations using only vanilla client-side files.

By combining date calculations with client storage variables, this project simulates complete database integrations entirely in memory, making it an ideal demonstration of layout responsiveness and animation capabilities.

The Power of Plants

Understanding why maintaining greenery supports our local ecosystem and aligns with SDG 15.

Through photosynthesis, plants absorb carbon dioxide and release oxygen. Certain species like the Snake Plant and Peace Lily excel at removing household air pollutants such as benzene, formaldehyde, and trichloroethylene from the atmosphere.

Horticultural therapies and daily interactions with flora have been proven to lower cortisol levels, reduce psychological stress, and increase focus and memory retention by up to 20% in study environments.

Sustainable Development Goal 15 seeks to protect and restore terrestrial ecosystems. Planting domestic gardens slows down local soil degradation, supports insect bio-diversity, and raises microclimate resilience against heat waves in cities.
